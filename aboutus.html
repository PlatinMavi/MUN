We invest in finding solutions to world's problems

DERMUN (Muammer Dereli Fen Lisesi Model United Nations) is a Model UN conference which will be held on May 13-14-15th, 2023 at Muammer Dereli Science High School, İzmit. We hope that this conference will bring many values to the delegates, including politics, general political issues in the world, international and individual relations and working principles of international organizations, especially the United Nations.
