Frequently asked questions

What is MUN

Model United Nations, also known as model UN or MUN, is an educational simulation in which students learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. At a MUN conference, students work as the representative of a country, organization, or person, and must solve a problem with other delegates from around the world.

What is the procedure of the conference?

Conference procedure will be the HARVARD procedure.

Where will DERMUN'23 be held?

DERMUN'23 will be held at Muammer Dereli Science Highschool Campus.

When will DERMUN'23 be held?

DERMUN'23 will be held on 12-14 June 2023. You can keep updated by following our instagram account @dermun23.

Is there a social event?

Yes, we are planning to have multiple social events. For more information follow our instagram account @dermun23.

What are the bank account details for the payment?

Our bank account details will be shared with you via email after you get a notice of acceptance from our Organization Team.

When is the last date for payments?

You are expected to make your payments within 3 business days from the moment you get an acceptance. İf you are not make your payments within 3 business days your application will be considered invalid.

How can I apply?

After carefully checking the deadlines, you can apply via the forms that can be found in the “Register Now” button at the home page.

How and when will I learn if I am accepted?

Within 7 days after the deadline for the application, you will receive an email notifying you that your application has been received. (Please notify us if you haven’t received it after 7 days.)

Are applications evaluated on a first-come, first-served basis?

Yes, applications are evaluated on a first-come, first-served basis meaning that first applications get evaluated first by the Secretariat. Therefore, there might be a greater chance of acceptance if you apply as early as possible to DERMUN ‘23.

Is there a dresscode ?

- Yes, all participants must wear formal clothes throughout the conference.

What will happen if I miss any sessions?

Delegates who miss 3 or more sessions will not be able to receive their participation certificate or rewards.

What is the schedule like for a participant?

The schedule will be announced in the following weeks, so please keep checking our website and our social media accounts regularly!
